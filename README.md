## About Ani Bot

Ani Bot is a feature-rich Discord bot made for anime lovers. It includes:

- Anime & character search (via Jikan/MAL API)
- Random anime picks & recommendations
- Weekly airing schedule & currently airing shows
- Anime memes from r/animemes
- Anime news & announcements
- Auto-posting news channels
- Meme & suggestion systems
